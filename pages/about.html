The AromaLux Journey

Founded in 2010, AromaLux began with a simple mission: to bring the world's finest fragrances to discerning customers who appreciate the art of perfumery.

Our journey started in a small atelier in Paris, where our founder, master perfumer Isabelle Laurent, combined her passion for rare botanicals with innovative scent creation techniques.

Today, we partner with over 100 premium fragrance houses worldwide, curating collections that represent the pinnacle of olfactory excellence.
